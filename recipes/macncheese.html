Ingredients

  • 1 box Kraft Macaroni & Cheese
  • 6 cups boiling water
  • 4 Tbsp butter
  • 1/4 cup 2% milk

Steps

  1. Add 6 cups of water to a pot and bring to a boil.
  2. Stir macaroni into boiling water. Let boil for 7 to 8 minutes or until macaroni is tender.
  3. Once your macaroni is tender, drain it and set it aside.
  4. Add your 4 Tbsp of butter to a hot pan then add your 1/4 cup of 2% milk.
  5. Once your butter has melted stir in the included cheese packet from the box.
  6. Stir the sauce into your drained macaroni.
