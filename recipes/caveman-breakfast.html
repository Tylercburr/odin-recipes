Caveman Breakfast

Description

Imagine this, you're deep in the backwoods of the High Uintah Mountain range. Its early, the cool mountain air bites through your jacket like a million little needles. You're in Heaven, the only thing that could make it better is a hot breakfast, crispy potatos and perfect eggs. You get to work chopping potatos and onions, then it hits you, you have absolutly no idea what you're doing. don't worry though, I can help.

Remember how I said I could help not even a few moments ago? Yeah..... I lied, me trying to cook is like a lobster trying to fly. I'll pretend until I hit the ground a little to fast. So heres one of those delusions, easy to make and straight forward. lets imagine we have wings shall we?

Ingredients

  • 2 diced potatos
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 diced onion
  • 1/2 diced green bell pepper
  • 2 strips diced bacon
  • 3 Tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup shredded chedder cheese
  • 1 Tbsp garlic salt
  • salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Pre-heat you pan then add your bacon, cook thoroughly then set aside
  2. Toss your butter into the same pan you used for your bacon and allow it to melt
  3. Add your potatos, onions and bell pepper. Cook until your potatos are golden brown
  4. Now add your cooked bacon, garlic powder, shredded cheese and eggs
  5. Cook until eggs are well done, then salt and pepper to taste
  6. take off your kids fairy wing costume before anyone sees you
